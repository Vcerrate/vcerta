{% raw %}UNIVERSITY OF TORONTO<br />Autonomous Space Robotics Lab:  <a rel="nofollow" target="blank" href="http://asrl.utias.utoronto.ca">http://asrl.utias.utoronto.ca</a><br />YouTube Channel:  <a rel="nofollow" target="blank" href="http://www.youtube.com/utiasASRL">http://www.youtube.com/utiasASRL</a><br /><br />This video shows an improvement to the popular RRT* planning algorithm that focusses the search area by eliminating some regions that cannot possibly improve the solution.
