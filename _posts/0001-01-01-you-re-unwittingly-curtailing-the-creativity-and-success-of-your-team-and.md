{% raw %}Have you ever wondered why some people seem to flourish in the workplace while others just...don’t? Chances are that neurodiversity has something to do with it and you’re missing out on some incredible ideas from a group of people that have different needs when it comes to environment, communication, and workday hours. Neurodiversities (like autism and ADHD) are the outcome of natural variations in the human genome and often result in a brain that innately excels at the type of thinking necessary for engineering and development. The caveat: neurodivergent brains require a unique work environment to thrive.
