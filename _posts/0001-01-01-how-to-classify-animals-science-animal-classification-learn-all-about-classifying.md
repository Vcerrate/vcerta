They place them in a hierarchy of groupings, beginning with the kingdom animalia and proceeding through phyla, classes, orders, families, genera and species. There are 2 main groups that animal are divided into, one is vertebrates (animals with a backbone) is further classified into five different classes—mammals, birds, fish, reptiles and amphibians.
