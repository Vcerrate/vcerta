Life hack with avocado<br />Avocados are not only delicious, they are also very healthy and rich in fats that are good for our diet. But everyone knows them for fast spoiling. Yes, it is hard to keep them in a fridge for a long time. Do you know how to keep a half of avocado fresh longer? We will tell you now. How to start a fire in an emergency situation<br />Here is a great hack that may actually save your life one day. If you are lost and do not have paper to lit the fire, check if you have some chips with you. How to open a bottle using paper<br />How many times have you been in this amazing situation: you bought a cold refreshing drink but can't find a bottle opener? No need to worry! How not to fall asleep at work or at school<br />Sometimes all of use are having these sleepy days when we just cannot concentrate on anything. A delicious chocolate muffin is done!
