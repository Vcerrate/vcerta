{% raw %}The Brazilian bank authority launched the new Instant Payment System, PIX, in November 2020. This new mobile payment system fits because Brazil is the fifth-largest internet and mobile phone economy worldwide! <br /><br />It works all the time, including the weekend and holidays, and is accessible to all. PIX is an improvement on the existent bank structure. Fast and secure. The Central Bank advised that all banks upgrade to the new PIX method.<br /><br />This new payment method is secure and enabled by QR codes, keys or manual filling in.{% endraw %}
