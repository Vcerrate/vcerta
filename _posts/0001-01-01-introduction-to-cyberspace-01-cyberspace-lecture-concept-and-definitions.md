{% raw %}Concept and definitions of cyberspace<br />Cyberspace refers to the virtual computer world, and more specifically, an electronic medium that is used to facilitate online communication.<br /><br />Cyberspace typically involves a large computer network made up of many worldwide computer subnetworks that employ TCP/IP protocol to aid in communication and data exchange activities.<br /><br />Cyberspace's core feature is an interactive and virtual environment for a broad range of participants.<br /><br />In the common IT lexicon, any system that has a significant user base or even a well-designed interface can be thought to be “cyberspace.”{% endraw %}
