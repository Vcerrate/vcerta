{% raw %}SCP Orientation is an archive of files of the SCP Foundation.<br /><br /><br /><br />Today we will be studying Item number SCP-603: Self-Replicating Computer Program, Object class: Safe.<br /><br /><br /> <br />SCP-603 is a self-replicating computer program capable of reproducing and extending its own source code.<br /> <br />When left to run uninterrupted, SCP-603 will generate prime numbers increasing from 2 and print them to the environment's standard output. Upon hitting a super-prime, there is a chance that a GPU-accelerated window will open. The contents of this window are varying. <br /> <br />Die [SCP-603-44]<br />Die presents itself as a text-based interactive adventure game. When Die starts up, the following passage is printed to the screen:<br />Your head pounds ever harder as you struggle through the jagged bramble. You gaze back through the smog at the silhouette of the old lighthouse to the north, the faintest glimmer of hope extinguished so violently by your foolish exploits. A distant and unattainable fantasy, you know you can never return.<br /> <br /> <br /><br /><br /><br /><br />Proceed to begin your training.<br /><br /><br /><br /> <br /><br />This video is derived from <a rel="nofollow" target="blank" href="https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/scp-603">https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/scp-603</a> and released under Creative Commons Sharealike 3.0.
