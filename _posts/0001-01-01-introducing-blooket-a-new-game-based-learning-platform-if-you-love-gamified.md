{% raw %}If you love gamified review sites like Kahoot, Quizziz, Gimkit, or Quizlet then you will love Blooket.<br /><br />Blooket is a new spin on the classroom review game.  With Blooket your students can review course content including vocabulary while playing a number of different game modes.  These modes can be played together as a class or assigned individually for homework.  <br /><br />Each review game introduces elements of strategy and includes a class leaderboard, making the review experience fun and engaging.<br /><br />Some game types are constantly changing as well, ensuring that students don't lose interest. <br /><br />Check out Blooket now:<br /><a rel="nofollow" target="blank" href="http://www.Blooket.com">http://www.Blooket.com</a><br /><br />* Special thanks to Senor Belles<br /> for providing the game footage:<br /><a rel="nofollow" target="blank" href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCZgpmNckmLzmV0EwCiMEZGA">https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCZgpmNckmLzmV0EwCiMEZGA</a>{% endraw %}
