{% raw %}Using Locker in Brussels Train Station<br /><br />If you're traveling through Brussels and have a lot of luggage, don't worry. There are lockers located in the train station for you to store your belongings while you explore the city. The lockers are easy to use. Just deposit the money and your given a receipt with a barcode, which you scan to open the locker when you return.{% endraw %}
