VERIVERY SERIES ‘O’ [ROUND 3 : WHOLE]<br />From the ‘FACE it’ series delving into the concept of ‘facing the many selves co-existing in oneself’ to the ‘SERIES ‘O’’ series and its exploration of the ‘darkness within’ that all dread facing, yet must, VERIVERY has developed a stable foundation for its world-building over its releases. The world-building now reaches its spectacular height through VERIVERY’s first full album [ROUND 3 : WHOLE].<br /><br />Hello Darkness, Learning How to Co-exist with It<br />Through the story strewn across SERIES ‘O’ VERIVERY had found O, a space of darkness, at a party that they were invited to by mysterious figures, eventually facing what was at its core. In [ROUND 3 : WHOLE], the story now moves on to focus on the struggle between those seeking to face the darkness and those trying to protect it, with the phrase ‘Hello Darkness’ becoming the key to the conflict.<br /><br />‘Hello Darkness’ also serves as the ultimate message that VERIVERY wishes to deliver through its entirety of SERIES ‘O’. In the end, ‘darkness’ is a necessity that one must learn to co-exist with in order to live on. However, the interpretation of this notion, as well as the means to do so, must be reached individually.<br /><br />‘It’s Coming, the Perfect Time’ – Finally, the Endgame!<br />The endgame is the point when conflicts come to a close. At the end of this battle within the darkness, a clear winner exists, yet both sides must continue on, learning to co-exist and maintaining peace from each of their own worlds.<br /><br />In this album, VERIVERY has managed to express this concept of the ‘endgame’ in a number of ways. The album cover depicts a circle reminiscent of fight rings to clearly showcase the album’s main message, while the antique elevator set introduces an air of mystery to the atmosphere. As well, the concept films connecting the new story to previous installments of the series make effective use of ambience in order to create suspense as if one was watching a horror film. Multiple action movie-like sequences portraying conflict and extravagant use of CG only add to enrichening the experience by creating a sensory explosion.<br /><br />첫 정규 앨범!
