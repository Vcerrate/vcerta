{% raw %}Accounting is the act of sorting and recording information. <br /> <br />Accounting procedures can help you manage the huge amount of information that a business can generate. Accounting records sort and categorize financial data into groups of information that are meaningful to stakeholders of the business.<br /> <br />A successful and active business may encounter hundreds of transactions in a single period. What's the best way to handle incoming data?<br /> <br />The efficiency of the accounting process starts with accurate and well-organized data; therefore a clear understanding of procedures is important.
