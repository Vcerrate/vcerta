{% raw %}Why is education important? What is our responsibility to future generations? What is our role, the private sector and the government in education? Dr. Mostapha Kalami Heris  answers these questions. Mostapha Kalami Heris is the CEO and founder of FaraDars. He recived his PhD from Khajeh Nasir Tusi University and has many articles and research in his scientific record. FaraDars is a familiar name among Iranians, which is one of the top online educational platforms. Now he and his colleagues are looking to expand an international version of FaraDars. This talk was given at a TEDx event using the TED conference format but independently organized by a local community.
