The dog mushers will inspire and be a great example for dog owners around the world in regards to training, nutrition, general welfare and guidelines for their own dogs.<br /><br />Rob Urbach, CEO of The Iditarod said: “We are proud to be a part of QRILL PAWS, along with the Femund Race and John Beargrease.
