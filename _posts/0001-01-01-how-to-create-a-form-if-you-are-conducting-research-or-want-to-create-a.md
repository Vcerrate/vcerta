Let’s see the steps to create Google Form.<br /><br />1. Open the Google Chrome browser.<br />2. Click on the Google apps icon at the top right.<br />3. Scroll down the apps list, and click on the Forms.<br />4. If you are not signed in, then type your email and click on the Next button.<br />5. Enter your Google account password, and click on the Next button.<br />6. After Signing in on the Google Forms, click on the Blank to create a new form.<br />7. Type the name of the Form, and write a short description for the purpose of the form.<br />8. Write your question in the Untitled Question box.<br />9. Start adding the choice of answers for the audience.<br />10. From the drop-down on the right side you can select the format for selection in the radio button or check box form.<br />11. If you want, you can add an image to a question that can relate to it.<br />12. To add more questions with a similar format style, you can click on the Duplicate icon.<br />13. Click in the question box to change the question.<br />14. You can also change the answer options by removing, editing, or adding the new answer option.<br />15. Click on the plus button icon on the right side to add the new question format.<br />16. You can import questions, add title and description, image, video, or a new section by clicking the icons on the right-side.<br />17. If you want to delete an unused question section, then click on the delete button of the Trash icon.<br />18. Once your form is ready, then click on the Send button at the top right.<br />19. You can email it, click on the get link tab, or click on the Embed HTML tag.<br />20.
