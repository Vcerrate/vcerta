{% raw %}In Q4, Rategain posted revenue at Rs 107 cr vs Rs 71.6 cr YoY. PAT came in at Rs 11 cr vs loss of Rs 5.8 cr in the year-ago period. Martech business unit saw a recurring revenue of 98.6%. DaaS business unit registered strong growth in its airline and OTA products.
