{% raw %}Shaping and Chaining are strategies in ABA therapy that enable the child to learn more complex behaviors. Chaining breaks the task down into a variety of smaller steps that are then taught to the child individually. Shaping involves reinforcing your child for engaging in behaviors that gradually lead to the desired goal.
