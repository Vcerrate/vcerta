{% raw %}On 1 February 2022 when the Property Practitioners Act and Regulations come into affect, the property sector is changing legislatively.  This means the EAAB is disbanded, almost all agreements and marketing material used by property professionals will require updating, and regulatory compliance requirements are enhanced. TPN Credit Bureau will be taking you through the Act and what will be expected from the property sector.{% endraw %}
