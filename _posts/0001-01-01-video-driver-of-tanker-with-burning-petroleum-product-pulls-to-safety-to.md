{% raw %}Providence intervened Friday evening to avert what could have resulted in a major disaster in Agbarho, a town near Warri in Ughelli North Local Government Area after a burning petroleum tanker went up in flames but was successfully removed from harming anyone.<br /><br />The driver of the tanker, Ejiro Otaorighobe, his father, Samuel as well as the owner of the tanker, Mr Chidiebere Ugo all appreciated God for intervening to avert the disaster.<br /><br />People are however praising the driver for his heroic and brave action in driving the burning truck to safety.<br /><br />What happened in Agbarho on Friday evening could best be described as a film director's delight.<br /><br />A burning petroleum truck zipped through the Agbarho axis of the popular road East-West road.<br /><br />But, this time it was real as the driver rushed through the town for a safe ground to park the vehicle before it explodes.<br /><br />Hundreds of people watched in awe and fear for the worst as the burning truck navigated its way towards the Agbarho Riverside.<br /><br />People gave way, many brought out their phones to record, apprehensive for the worst with every ticking moment.<br /><br />But, the explosion that would have claimed lives, destroyed houses, vehicles and other infrastructure did not happen.<br /><br />Thanks to the bravery and courage displayed by the driver of the ruck, Ejiro Otarigho.<br /><br />Narrating what happened, Otarigho said he had just offloaded the petrol in the tanker and was leaving the petrol station when he was alerted of fire emanating from the tanker.<br /><br />According to him, he did a quick-thinking and decided to take the truck to a place where there will be minimal damage.<br /><br />For him and many people, this was a classic movie case as in about twenty minutes the tanker had cleared several major streets and was settled by the Agbarho Riverside.<br /><br />Ejiro said he understood the enormous responsibility on his shoulder as he drove the burning tanker through the streets.<br /><br />He explained that at any moment the residual petrol in the truck could have ignited killing him instantly and causing massive death and destruction.{% endraw %}
