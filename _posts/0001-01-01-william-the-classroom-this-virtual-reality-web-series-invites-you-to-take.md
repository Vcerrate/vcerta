{% raw %}This virtual reality web series invites you to take on the role of William, a twelve-year-old Indigenous boy who is taken from his family and placed in an Indian residential school against his will.<br /><br />For one revealing moment, you will be this child. Not only will you witness the events he saw and experienced-you will live through these events. You will feel what he felt, act as he acted. While in William's skin, you will experience the unthinkable. <br /><br />Through fiction, you will see reality.<br /><br />WILLIAM puts technology and cinema in the service of education, truth, and reconciliation.<br /><br />(The video you are about to watch was shot in a non-existent language to replicate the effect felt by students in Indian residential Schools.){% endraw %}
