{% raw %}POLICIES<br />It is defined as organisation’s general response to a particular problem or situation.<br />It is the organisation’s own way of handling the problems.<br />Policies are made at very level because the managers at every level needs to decide or predetermined way of handling a situation.<br />Policy act as a guide to take the decision in unexpected situations.<br />Policy formation always encourages initiative of employees because employees have to deal with the situations and the way of handling the situation is decided in consultation of employees.<br />Then they will be able to handle the situation in a much better way.{% endraw %}
