{% raw %}This series of three webinars will support schools to develop an implementation plan to improve outcomes for disadvantaged children and young people at SEN Support.<br /><br />Sessions will provide resources to support schools in developing and evaluating their implementation plan as well as using the evidence-informed graduated approach to SEND support in schools.<br /><br />This series aims to:<br /><br />- Highlight the importance of inclusion being at the core of development planning in schools<br />- Provide evidence from schools who have developed their inclusive practice<br />- Support leaders in schools in implementing their development plans and monitoring impact<br /><br />The series will feature three sessions spread over the Summer and Autumn terms to allow attendees time to develop and implement strategies:<br /><br />Part 1, Monday 21st June, 4.00-5.00 PM: Development Planning for Inclusion - Featuring guest speaker Marc Rowland.<br /><br />Part 2, Wednesday 14th July, 4.00-5.00 PM: Developing and Embedding Inclusive Practice - Featuring case studies from practitioners and a presentation from Dr Jim Rogers.<br /><br />Part 3, Tuesday 14th September, 4.00-5.00 PM: Implementing, Monitoring and Evaluating - Featuring guest speaker Marc Rowland.<br /><br />This webinar was recorded on 21st June 2021.
