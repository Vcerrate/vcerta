{% raw %}In 2021, considerable changes were introduced in the areas of VAT and Authorized Representation (AR). Since 1 July 2021, new European VAT rules for business-to-consumers (B2C) transactions are applied, impacting suppliers of services, goods, marketplaces and postal couriers.<br /><br />As of 16 July 2021, new regulation on product conformity and market surveillance for businesses selling to the European Union are applied. The European Union published Regulation (EU) 2019/1020 on Market Surveillance and Compliance of Products on 20 June 2019. The regulation aims to protect customers’ health and safety, the environment and other public interests by improving and modernizing market surveillance.<br /><br />During the webinar ‘European e-commerce in 2021, new VAT and authorized representative rules as per July this year’ two specialists, René Schilder, Managing Director Focus on VAT, and Han Zuyderwijk, Founder Cemarking.net, elaborated on this.<br /><br />#VAT #AR #authorizedrepresentative #ecommerce #regulations #b2c{% endraw %}
