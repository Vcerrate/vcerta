{% raw %}While he was conceptualizing “You Won’t Be Alone,” writer-director Goran Stolevski wanted to create a brand new folk tale. Drawing from his Macedonian upbringing, Stolevski’s film tells the story of a shape-shifting witch known as the Wolf-Eateress, who terrorizes an 18th century Balkan village, searching for blood.
