{% raw %}Could it be that aliens are trying to communicate with us right now, but we're just not listening in the right way? They might have chosen a specific frequency, time or method of communication that we just never thought to try. In game theory, two players can co-operate towards a common goal even without communication. The idea is called a Schelling point - today we'll explore what this is and how it affects our search for life in the Universe.<br /><br />Written &amp; presented by Prof David Kipping.
