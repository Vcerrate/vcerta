{% raw %}The robes that Pope Francis will wear during his visit to Kenya have been stitched in the country in a sprawling informal settlement in the capital Nairobi. A team of tailors stitched three special vestments, each for everyday that the pontiff is in the country. The tailors have termed the work a spiritual duty and an honor.  The head of the Catholic Church will be in Kenya beginning Wednesday next week for his first visit in Africa.
