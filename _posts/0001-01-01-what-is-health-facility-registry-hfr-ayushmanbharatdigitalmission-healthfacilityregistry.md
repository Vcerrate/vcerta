The Health Facility Registry (HFR) is a comprehensive repository of health facilities of the nation across different systems of medicine. HFR is open to registration of all health facilities in India providing healthcare services across both modern and traditional systems of medicine such as Modern Medicine (Allopathy), Dentistry, Physiotherapy, Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha, Homeopathy and Sowa-Rigpa systems of medicine.
