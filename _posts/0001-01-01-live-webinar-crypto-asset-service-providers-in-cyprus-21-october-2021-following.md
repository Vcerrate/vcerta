{% raw %}Following the recent transposition of AMLD V into Cyprus Law which brings into scope providers offering services related to crypto-assets, and the subsequent issuance of CySEC’s relevant Directive and Policy Statement, MAP S.Platis presented a free live webinar session where our expert consultants and associate discussed this popular new topic providing participants, among others, with an overview of the new regulatory framework in Cyprus and the EU with respect to Crypto-Asset Service Providers (CASPs), the CySEC registration process, important issues that CASPs should be aware of as well as which technologies and solutions CASPs can use to comply with the relevant AML requirements and meet CySEC’s expectations.{% endraw %}
