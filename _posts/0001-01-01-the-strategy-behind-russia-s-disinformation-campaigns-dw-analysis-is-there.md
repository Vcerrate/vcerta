{% raw %}Is there a playbook for how the Kremlin spreads disinformation when it’s at the center of a global outcry? We have found three tactics that it has used again and again:<br />Spreading confusion, with massive amounts of misleading information. The aim appears to be to muddy the waters for those who want to know more.<br />Turn the allegations around. Rather than defend itself, it shifts the focus through attacks, bold responses of strength, and disinformation.<br />The Kremlin's spokespeople also tend to attack opposing views – moving the focus away from the allegations themselves. They like to imply or point out that the West is just as bad as it accuses Russia of being.
